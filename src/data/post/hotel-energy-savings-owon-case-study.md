## The Challenge

The hotel chain faced rising energy costs across its 200-room property. Traditional HVAC systems ran 24/7 regardless of occupancy, wasting up to 40% of energy. Manual thermostat adjustments by staff were slow and inconsistent. Guest temperature complaints averaged 12 per day, each taking 2+ hours to resolve.

## The Solution

The hotel deployed OWON's Smart Hotel Energy Management system:

- **PC341-3M16S Multi-Circuit Monitors** installed per floor to track energy consumption at a granular level
- **PCT513 Touchscreen Thermostats** in every room with occupancy detection and automated temperature control
- **EdgeEco IoT Platform** providing a centralized dashboard for facility managers with real-time alerts

The system uses ZigBee mesh networking for reliable in-building communication, requiring no new wiring.

## How It Works

1. **Sub-metering**: PC341 monitors track energy consumption per floor, wing, and individual room
2. **Smart Thermostats**: PCT513 units detect occupancy and adjust temperature automatically
3. **Central Dashboard**: EdgeEco provides real-time analytics, energy reports, and automated alerts
4. **Automated Rules**: Temperature setbacks activate during unoccupied hours and guest checkout

## Results

- **30% reduction** in HVAC energy costs — saving approximately $4,200 per month
- **15-minute response** to temperature complaints (down from 2+ hours)
- **80% fewer guest complaints** about room temperature
- **Full visibility** into energy consumption patterns by floor and time of day
