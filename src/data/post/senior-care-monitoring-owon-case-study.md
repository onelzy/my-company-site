## The Challenge

The 200-bed senior care facility relied on hourly night rounds to check on residents — a labor-intensive process that missed critical events between visits. Wearable emergency devices were often refused by elderly residents due to discomfort and stigma. Staff burnout was high, and families worried about response times during emergencies.

## The Solution

OWON's Senior Care Monitoring System was deployed across all 200 beds:

- **SPM-915 Smart Sleep Monitors** placed under mattresses — completely non-contact, no wearables needed
- **Emergency Call Buttons** wall-mounted in every room and portable options for active residents
- **AiJuan Platform** with mobile app for caregivers and web dashboard for facility managers

The system uses ZigBee mesh networking for reliable whole-facility coverage with zero new wiring.

## How It Works

1. **Continuous Monitoring**: SPM-915 pads track sleep quality, heart rate, and respiratory rate without any wearable devices
2. **Emergency Detection**: Falls, prolonged inactivity, and abnormal vitals trigger instant alerts
3. **Caregiver Notification**: AiJuan app sends push notifications with room number, event type, and resident vitals
4. **Analytics Dashboard**: Facility managers see trends, compliance reports, and staffing optimization recommendations

## Results

- **90% alert accuracy** — fewer false alarms, faster genuine responses
- **3-minute average response** to emergency events (down from 12+ minutes)
- **40% reduction** in night-shift staffing needs through automated monitoring
- **92% family satisfaction** score — up from 68% before deployment

> "The SPM-915 sleep monitors transformed our night shift. We went from hourly room checks to targeted responses. Our residents sleep better and our staff stress levels dropped dramatically."
> — Nursing Director

## Key Takeaways

For senior care operators, non-contact monitoring is the key to adoption — residents don't need to wear or interact with any devices. The combination of passive monitoring and active alert buttons provides comprehensive coverage without the stigma of medical wearables. Night-shift optimization alone delivers ROI within months.
